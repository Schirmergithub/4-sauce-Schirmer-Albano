About Sauce


The Heart of Andover

Sauce is a restaurant located in the center of downtown andover near the traintracks. If you're in the mood for a good burger or wings with some killer sauce then Sauce is the place for you. They've got a couple TV's for entertainment and an outdoors area. Overall, Sauce is a great place to hangout and get a bite to eat.
